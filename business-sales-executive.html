Business Sales Executive


As a Business Sales Executive at MFL Enterprises, you will play a pivotal role in driving growth and expanding our presence in the transportation dispatch industry. Your primary responsibility will be to identify and pursue new business opportunities, forge strategic partnerships, and nurture relationships with key clients. Leveraging your expertise in sales, you will develop and execute innovative sales strategies, targeting both local and national markets, to achieve revenue targets and maximize market share. Collaborating closely with cross-functional teams, you will gather market intelligence, analyze industry trends, and develop tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of our clients. With your exceptional communication and negotiation skills, you will build strong customer relationships, deliver compelling sales presentations, and negotiate contracts that drive mutual success.
